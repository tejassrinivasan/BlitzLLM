# Examples & Use Cases

Discover the full power of Blitz through comprehensive examples that showcase our advanced NBA analysis capabilities. From simple player queries to complex multi-dimensional statistical analysis, these examples demonstrate the sophisticated insights possible with our natural-language sports query agent.

**Data Coverage**: Complete professional basketball history from 1946-2025, including BAA (1946-1949) and all NBA seasons. Comprehensive player stats, team performance, play-by-play events, betting lines, and advanced metrics across 79 years of basketball history.

        ```bash Basic Statistical Analysis
        curl -X POST "https://blitzagent.onrender.com/analyze" \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_BLITZ_API_KEY" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -d '{
            "query": "What are Jayson Tatum shooting percentages and usage rate this season?",
            "extra_context": "Include FG%, 3P%, FT%, eFG%, TS%, and compare to league averages"
          }'
        ```
