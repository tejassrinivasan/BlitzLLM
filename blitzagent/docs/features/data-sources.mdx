## Data Source Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Historical NBA Data">

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Web Scraping">
    **Real-time Information**
    
    Current sports news, injury reports, trade information, roster changes, and breaking developments from reliable sports sources.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Betting Market Data">
    **Multi-Sportsbook Coverage**
    
    Historical and current betting lines, prop markets, odds movements, and outcomes from major sportsbooks including player props, team totals, and game spreads.
  </Step>
</Steps>
